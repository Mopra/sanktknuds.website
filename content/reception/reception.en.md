After many months of work, we are proud to finally open the doors to Sankt Knuds Brasseri & Bar on Ryesgade 29 in the heart of Aarhus.

To mark the occasion, we are hosting an open reception. Everyone is welcome to drop by, see our new restaurant and bar, meet the team and experience the setting we look forward to filling with good moments for many years to come.

Light food and drinks will be served throughout the event. We look forward to welcoming you, showing you around and raising a glass to the start of a new chapter.
