Sankt Knuds Brasseri & Bar sits at Ryesgade 29 in the heart of central Aarhus — a few minutes' walk from the Latin Quarter, Aarhus Cathedral and the pedestrian high street.

Arriving by train, it's a short walk up Ryesgade from Aarhus Central Station. The light rail (Letbanen) and city buses stop nearby, and there are several public car parks within walking distance. Bike racks are right outside the door.

We're open from lunch until late every day except Sunday. Reservations are recommended on Fridays and Saturdays.
